## 2024-05-24 - Double Tactile Feedback on Buttons
**Learning:** Adding complex animations using `duration-500` breaks the rule of keeping animations under 300ms. Quick, snappy interactions (`duration-200`) feel much more responsive and premium for a mobile-first PWA. Also, applying `group-active` correctly triggers internal icon animations simultaneously with the outer container's `active:scale-95`.
**Action:** When applying tactile feedback, stick to `duration-200` max and use `group`/`group-active` on the parent to drive the nested elements. This gives that satisfying "double tactile" feedback without latency.
